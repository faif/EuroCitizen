EuropeanOntoCitizens will educate, support and help European citizens and promote efficacy of society. The identity of a European citizen is two-fold. One is the physical/biological identity related to his DNA, and the other is related to the European environment and culture. Similarly, the identity of the EuropeanOntoCitizen will be two-fold. One side refers to its existence and the other to the rules of actions that govern the interactions and relationships among them." [lang: en]
The EuroCitizen ontology uses a modified version of the New Testament Names (NTN) ontology (http://www.semanticbible.com/ntn/ntn-overview.html), which can be downloaded from http://www.semanticbible.com/ntn/ntn-view.html.
